Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Ankit Dixit Physics Classes. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our website is a static, informational platform. We respect your privacy and do not collect any personal data automatically. We do not use user registration forms, login portals, or payment gateways on this website. You can browse our website completely anonymously.

2. Information You Voluntarily Provide

We only receive personal information (such as your name, phone number, or email address) if you voluntarily choose to contact us directly through:

  • WhatsApp messages
  • Direct Phone Calls or SMS
  • Email communications
  • Direct messages on our Social Media profiles (Instagram, Facebook)

Any information you share through these channels is used strictly to respond to your queries, provide batch details, or assist you with coaching-related information. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact details with any third-party marketing agencies.

3. External Links & Social Media

Our website contains links to our official social media pages, including YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. Clicking on these links will redirect you to their respective apps or websites.

Please note that we have no control over how these third-party platforms collect or use your data. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook before interacting with them.

4. Cookies

Since this is a simple informational website, we do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels to track your browsing behavior across the internet.

5. Changes & Future Updates to This Policy

Currently, our website operates as a static informational platform. However, as we grow, we may introduce new features in the future (such as online batch enrollment, user accounts, or payment gateways).

When such features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect how we securely collect and process the necessary data (like payment information). Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
